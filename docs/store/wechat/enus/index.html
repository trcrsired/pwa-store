Add WeChat Quick Actions to Your Home Screen

WeChat provides official shortcuts—My QR Code, Money, and Scan—that you can place directly on your home screen. This makes accessing them significantly faster.

1. Long‑press the WeChat app icon

A menu will appear showing My QR Code, Money, and Scan.

2. Long‑press one of the actions

For example, long‑press My QR Code. You will be able to drag it to your home screen.

3. Place it on your home screen

Drop the icon anywhere on your home screen. You now have a direct shortcut to that WeChat feature.

Tip: If dragging does not work, check whether your phone allows apps to create home‑screen shortcuts. Some Chinese Android vendors disable this permission by default.
